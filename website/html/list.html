Recipe for Cinnamon Rolls

Ingredients

For the dough:

  • 1 cup warm milk
  • 2 teaspoon yeast
  • 3 cups bread floor

For the filling:

  • 2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/4 cup butter

Instructions

  1. Mix the milk with the yeast, sugar, eggs
  2. Melt the butter and add to the mixture
  3. Kneat the dough for 10 minutes
